## Academic Disclaimer
MindPolis is a technological platform designed to map theoretical models of political and moral psychology. The assessments and resulting "Cognitive Archetypes," "Topographies," and "Historical Correlates" are generated via probabilistic mathematical modeling heavily influenced by (but not officially endorsed by) established academic frameworks including the Moral Foundations Theory (MFT) and the Schwartz Theory of Basic Human Values.

These results are strictly exploratory and educational. They **do not** constitute clinical psychological diagnoses, absolute ideological definitions, or definitive moral judgments.

## 'Philosophical Resonance' Guardrails
When comparing a user's scoring vector to a historical or living political figure, MindPolis indicates a *pattern resemblance* based exclusively on pre-coded theoretical dimensions. 

This **does not** imply:
- Moral equivalence between the user and the figure.
- A holistic endorsement of the figure's actions, policies, or historical legacy.
- That the user "is" that specific politician.

MindPolis maintains a strict "No Monsters" protocol; universally recognized dictators and perpetrators of mass atrocities are permanently excluded from the comparison database.

## Privacy & Anonymity
We do not require Personally Identifiable Information (PII) to complete an assessment. Email addresses (where provided for saving results) are kept strictly confidential. IP addresses and geographical headers are temporarily aggregated strictly for macro-population baseline tracking and are decoupled from personal narrative profiles.
